Key places to visit

Bohali

Perched on a hill overlooking the island's capital, Zakynthos Town, Bohali offers visitors a breathtaking vista of the Ionian Sea and the town below. The village exudes traditional Greek charm, with its narrow winding streets, whitewashed buildings adorned with vibrant flowers, and quaint tavernas serving local delicacies.

Strada Marina hotel

On the other hand, if you stay in this amazing hotel, you can spend the entire day relaxing on the hotel itself. You can eat the complete breakfast, Strada Marina has to offer, by the rooftop pool. In the night, the cozy roof bar is open and ready to serve cocktails that can be enjoyed alongside the view of the port.
